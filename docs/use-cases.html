Every Platform, Liberated

Every major platform represents a content type being held hostage by a centralized algorithm and a 15–30% revenue extraction. Here's what each looks like when freed by protocol + agents.

The Pattern

Each service is small, protocol-native, agent-accessible, stablecoin-settled, and user-sovereign. When a user engages with one, their agent can introduce them to others.

Credit Card (familiar) → Stablecoin Settlement (fast, programmable) → Provider Receives (lower fees, instant) ↓ Over time: direct stablecoin payments — the card step becomes optional

Implementations

Active Development

Good Vibes — The Sovereign Scroll

Short-form video & audio • Replaces TikTok / Reels / Shorts

Personal Algorithm Engine (PAE) — a cognitive environment designer that gives individuals control over the algorithms that shape their content consumption. User defines their own algorithm: content weights, emotional filters, session arcs. No rage-bait unless they want it. No ads unless they're organic to their interests. Session caps that actually work because the whole experience is designed to serve the user, not extract their attention.

The test: A man opens the app at 5 AM. His algorithm composes a 15-minute session that makes him feel like a warrior. He keeps coming back every morning. The match is lit.

Stanzas — The Reading Revolution

Text: poems, stories, chapters, books • Replaces Kindle / Amazon Publishing

A TikTok-like viewer for text. Swipe through poems, short stories, a chapter of a novel. Background music. Audio mode with AI-narrated voices. Decentralized publishing where authors earn 95%+ instead of 30–65%.

Amazon takes 35–65% of ebook revenue. They control search, ranking, and discovery. Stanzas eliminates the gatekeeper entirely. The protocol IS the marketplace.

Concept

Sovereign Kids — The Parental Algorithm

Video, games, interactive media • Replaces YouTube Kids / Roblox

Parents own the algorithm — not YouTube, not Roblox. Granular controls that actually work because filtering happens at the consumer level. Content is enriched with LLM-powered safety tagging, and the parent's agent enforces their specific rules.

Currently parents have two options: total lockdown or blind trust. Web 4.0 gives them a third: informed, configurable sovereignty.

Concept

The Open Shelf — PWA Marketplace

Progressive Web Apps • Replaces App Store / Google Play

Apple takes 30%. Google takes 30%. PWAs bypass this entirely — web apps that install locally, no app store required. A protocol-based app registry where developers publish, consumers discover through their agent, and payment settles in stablecoins.

Apple itself suggested PWAs as an alternative to App Store limitations. The EU has fined Apple €500M for DMA non-compliance on browser restrictions.

Concept

YOMO — The Direct Market

Physical & digital products • Replaces Amazon Marketplace

Sellers publish product metadata to the protocol. Consumers' agents discover and compare across all sellers simultaneously. Payment settles peer-to-peer. No referral fees. No algorithmic suppression of competitors.

Amazon's power comes from controlling discovery AND fulfillment. Fulfillment is logistics (hard). Discovery is information — and protocols + agents solve information problems.

Health Dashboard — Sovereign Services

Health data & provider interactions • Replaces fragmented portals

Instead of 6 healthcare portals with 6 passwords, your agent assembles a unified dashboard. Your health data lives on your machine. Providers access what you authorize, when you authorize it.

The Transition Path

Web 4.0 succeeds by not asking permission. The user downloads an app. It looks familiar. It feels good. Behind the scenes, sovereignty happens.

Stage 1

Familiar App

Looks like what they know. Video feed. Swipe. Clean UI. They set preferences and use it.

Stage 2

Value Discovery

No unwanted ads. Content matches interests. Session caps work. They feel better after using it.

Stage 3

Savings

Creators: "I keep 95%?" Consumers: "4% cheaper without platform tax?" The economics sell themselves.

Stage 4

Sovereignty

Export profile. Connect providers. Adjust algorithm at dimension level. "I own this."

Stage 5

Ecosystem

Developers build providers. Creators publish directly. Web 4.0 becomes a movement — but it started as an app.

Build the Product. Prove the Value. Extract the Protocol.

Nobody cares about protocols. They adopt products. HTTP won because browsers were useful. SEP wins if Good Vibes is better than TikTok.
